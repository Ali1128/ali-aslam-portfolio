GenAI workflow approach

Use emerging tools without losing production accountability.

I use Gemini and Codex to accelerate exploration, debugging, documentation and interface iteration. I remain responsible for the production problem, workflow architecture, domain rules, test cases and final judgment.

For AI-assisted creative or media workflows, I separate suggestions from approvals, show the evidence behind a recommendation, document known limits and route uncertain output to a person. The goal is useful support for artists and operators, not an automated claim of creative ownership.
